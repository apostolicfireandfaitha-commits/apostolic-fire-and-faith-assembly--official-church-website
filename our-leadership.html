Meet Our Leadership

Pastor Victor Oluwawalehinmi, our Lead Pastor, is a prophetic voice for restoration and revival. With a passion for the Holy Spirit and a heart for winning souls, he delivers powerful teachings that ignite faith and produce miracles.

Our leadership team is supported by a dedicated group of ministers and women of power who oversee various departments and ministries, ensuring growth, accountability, and spiritual formation across the body.

Under the leadership of Pastor Victor Oluwawalehinmi, Apostolic Fire and Faith Assembly continues to expand its reach, impacting lives throughout Nigeria and beyond.

The Apostolic Fire and Faith Assembly is supported by a global team of Pastors and Ministers who are committed to advancing God's kingdom across the nations. Our leadership team brings together diverse gifts, experiences, and callings, all united under the vision of seeing lives transformed by the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
